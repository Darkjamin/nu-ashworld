Statistics

Your level is a measure of your experience and abilities in the game, and is governed by your current XP.

The maximum level is 99.

Affects:

  • MaxHitpoints
  • LevelRequirement (Perks usually have one associated with them, also some Quests)

On each level-up, you'll gain SkillPoints to upgrade your Skills with.

Every three levels (if you didn't pick the Skilled trait, in which case every four levels) you'll be able to permanently add a new Perk to your character.

The amount of XP needed to achieve a level is determined via formula:

XP=(Level∗(Level−1)/2)×1000 \mathit{XP} = (\mathit{Level}*(\mathit{Level}-1)/2) \times 1000 XP=(Level∗(Level−1)/2)×1000
