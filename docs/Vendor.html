Vendor

11th May 2021 at 1:31am

Vendor is a NPC you can Trade with. They are located in several Locations:

  • Hakunin in Arroyo
  • MaidaBuckner in Klamath
  • Flick in Den
  • Jo in Modoc
  • HappyHarry in VaultCity

The locations with a vendor are shown on the Map with full opacity, compared to locations without a vendor which are semi-transparent.
