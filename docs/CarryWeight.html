CarryWeight

24th April 2021 at 3:15pm
FutureContent Statistics

Carry weight is the amount of weight your character can hold in its inventory (sum of weights of all the items) before they will become Overencumbered and lose movement speed.

In-game description:

The maximum amount of equipment your character can carry, in pounds.

In normal circumstances, carry weight is calculated using this formula: CarryWeight=25+Strength×25 \mathit{CarryWeight} = 25 + \mathit{Strength} \times 25 CarryWeight=25+Strength×25

If the character possesses the SmallFrame trait, it instead changes (FutureContent) to: CarryWeight=25+Strength×15 \mathit{CarryWeight} = 25 + \mathit{Strength} \times 15 CarryWeight=25+Strength×15

Affects:

  • MovementSpeed (movement on the map costs more Ticks) (FutureContent)

Is affected by:

  • Strength attribute (see formulas above)
  • SmallFrame trait (see formulas above)
  • PackRat perk (+50) (FutureContent)
  • StrongBack perk (+50 per rank) (FutureContent)
