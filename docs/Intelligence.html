Intelligence

25th April 2021 at 11:26pm
SPECIAL

One of the SPECIAL attributes. As such, its value is always an integer between 1 and 10 inclusive.

In-game description:

Knowledge, wisdom and the ability to think quickly. A high Intelligence is important for any character.

Affects:

  • SkillPoints gained per level
  • HealingRate statistic
  • FirstAid skill
  • Doctor skill
  • Science skill
  • Repair skill
  • Outdoorsman skill

Is affected by:

  • Gifted trait (+1)
