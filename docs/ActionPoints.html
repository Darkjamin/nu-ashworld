ActionPoints

26th April 2021 at 7:39pm
Statistics

Action points (AP) allow for actions during Combat: moving, attacking, reloading (FutureContent) and so on.

They are determined from the Agility attribute using this formula:

ActionPoints=5+⌊Agility2⌋ \mathit{ActionPoints} = 5 + \lfloor\frac{\mathit{Agility}}{2}\rfloor ActionPoints=5+⌊2Agility​⌋

In case the player has the Bruiser trait, they get a 2 AP penalty.
