- Strength (ST)
  - if you don't meet weapon's min. ST requirement, aim accuracy is penalized (-20% per missing ST point)
- Perception (PE)
  - additional dialogue options
  - how far you're from enemies in random encounters
- Endurance (EN)
- Charisma (CH)
  - num of companions (CH 2 => 1 companion, 4->2, 6->3, 8->4, 10-> 5)
- Intelligence (IN)
- Agility (AG)
- Luck (LK)

At beginning: all skills start at 5, you have extra 5 free points to distribute, you can set stuff in between 1-10.
